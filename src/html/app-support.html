In this electronic era, customers expect 24x7 support for the products and the services they use. Support methodology

  • Includes everything from analysing and assessing the application to planning manpower and application management
  • We understand the client environment and maintain the agreed SLAs
  • We monitor resource utilization and work on daily, weekly and monthly reports, and have monthly reviews with clients to ensure stability and zero unplanned downtime for any application.
  • We help build knowledge base

Our application maintenance services are cost-effective. Maintenance services include customizing applications with new functional/technical requirements, software upgrade and optimization, and resolving performance issues.

Why choose us ?

We strive to achieve zero-incident status for all our customers by taking the relationship beyond the contract through these three values:

  • Visibility – We provide real-time visibility for all current issues, and are committed to delivering clients’ stated goals and objectives.
  • Momentum – We provide automation and optimization across applications by eliminating non-value additions within the business and cutting down on wasteful efforts.
  • Merit – We simplify the IT viewpoint through continuous improvement of clients’ application footprint and regular assessments towards reviewing current and target states.
